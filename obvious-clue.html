What's the best way to describe this page?

Title tags are the easiest SEO tag to update and have a huge effect on the page.

You can think of title tags as what sets the topic for the page. You'll want to throw in the correct keywords that you want your page to rank for. In the case of this website, I totally cheated and wanted to rank for a keyword that didn't exist "myseoloveletter". So that was immensely easy to rank for. Make sure your title tags are targeting a unique keyword space that isn't being targeted elsewhere on the website.
