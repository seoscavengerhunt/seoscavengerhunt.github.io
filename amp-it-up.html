SEO Challenge 6

Google can see text just fine. What about pictures?

AMP AMP AMP AMP AMP

AMP is a specific way of rendering your site that makes it load much faster. It's also endorsed by Google and any page can have an "AMP" version of the page, that Google will crawl and store on their CDN. Your page will then get a special icon in mobile search results(the lightning bolt icon you saw on the previous page) and when a user clicks on an AMPed search result, they'll load a page in under a second. There's a lot of nuances that go on with AMP, and it's much more complicated, but looking at the big picture, AMP pages load up in under a second and it looks like magic

I have a love/hate relationship with AMP. It's probably the most challenging product that I've worked on here, and it's taken over a year and a half to get the product dialed in, get the infrastructure up to speed and get the data in the right spot. I cannot thank everyone who's worked on AMP and has made it happen at Airbnb. I truly believe that it's a great step forward for Airbnb and everyone that made it happen are rock stars. There are way too many people to thank for this, as AMP's timeline has spanned two different reorgs, managers, engineers and data scientists.

Check out AMP if you have time. Pull out your phone, go to Google, and search "airbnb san francisco".
