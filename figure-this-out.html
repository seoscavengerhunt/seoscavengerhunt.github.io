What's the title of a page?

Androids dream of electric sheep and robots read the robots.txt

The robots.txt is a file that you place on the root of your website. It tells search engines where it can go and where it isn't allowed to go. It's quite an important file, if you configure it wrong, you'll tell Google to stop crawling your website. It's also an effective tool to stop Google from crawling pages on your website that don't matter and/or don't drive meaningful traffic.
