Meta descriptions have no direct impact on how you rank, but have a large secondary impact

Googlebot and search engine spiders don't use the meta description as a signal to rank your page, but the text that is displayed will affect how users interact with your result. If your meta description is attractive, or contains keywords that they're looking for, they're much more likely to click through to it.

When I first called my parents to tell them that I was accepting the offer at Airbnb, my dad had no idea what Airbnb was and what they did. He called me back a couple hours later and told me, "Brian, I think this is a good company you should take the offer." He then proceeded to tell me that he had done a lot of research and had printed out the Wikipedia article so that my mom could read it.
